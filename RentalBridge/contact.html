Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my property on Rental Bridge? +
Register as a property owner, complete your profile, and click on "Add Property" in your dashboard. Fill in all the required details, upload photos, and submit for approval.
Is there any fee for listing properties? +
Basic property listing is completely free. We offer premium features for enhanced visibility at affordable rates.
How long does property approval take? +
Our team reviews all property listings within 24-48 hours. You'll receive a notification once your property is approved.
Can I contact property owners directly? +
Yes! Use the inquiry form on any property listing to send a message directly to the owner via email.
What if I face technical issues? +
Contact our technical support team via email or phone. We're available Mon-Sat, 9 AM - 7 PM to help resolve any issues.
